# Trust Audit Heuristics

Upgrade severity when a problem is:
- hard to notice until damage is done
- hard to undo
- privacy-sensitive
- embarrassing if discovered later
- likely to produce words like “creepy”, “tricked”, “shady”, or “broke my stuff”

Common trust-killers:
- asking for permissions before explaining why
- mutating files without preview, consent, or bulk undo
- vague pricing around credits / usage / renewals / BYOK
- unclear cloud processing or provider fallback
- products that look “idle and healthy” while actually dead
- recovery paths that technically exist but are too hidden to matter
